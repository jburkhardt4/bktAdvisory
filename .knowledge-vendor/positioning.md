## Positioning statement
**John Burkhardt is the bridge between the Salesforce ecosystem and enterprise AI** —
turning platform and AI complexity into a predictable growth engine for revenue teams.

## Identity
- **Eyebrow / title:** `Salesforce + Enterprise AI Architect`
- **Role line:** `Founder & Principal Consultant`
- **Hero headline (chosen):** *Where the Salesforce ecosystem meets enterprise AI.*
- **Hero headline (alt, clarity-forward):** *Turn Salesforce and AI complexity into a predictable growth engine.*

## Four pillars
1. **Intersection** — Salesforce depth × enterprise-AI fluency; few operate credibly in both.
2. **Complexity → Clarity** — translate platform/AI sprawl into a clear, staged plan.
3. **Executive goals → Technical delivery** — own the path from boardroom outcome to shipped system.
4. **Predictable, compounding revenue** — RevOps that builds durable pipeline, not one-off wins.

## Lexicon
- **System of record** = Salesforce. **System of action** = AI agents. **System of growth** = RevOps.
- Voice: senior, precise, outcome-first, calm. Speak to revenue/operations leaders.
- **Banned words:** operationalize, leverage (verb), seamless, synergize, cutting-edge, supercharge.
